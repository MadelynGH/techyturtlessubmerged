Tips and Tricks

You can help too! Here are some things you can do to help:

  1. Put fertilizer at a appropriate time, don't put your fertilizer before it will rain, otherwise the fertilizer will runoff.
  2. If you're getting new grass or plants, try to get ones that need less fertilizer. Try to get natural fertilizers as well, such as dung and compost.
  3. Spread the word! Tell your friends and family about the problem!
  4. Try to get fertilizer that contains a safe or less amount of nitrogen and/or phosphorous.
  5. When applying fertilizer, mix it well! Make sure it is nicely underground.
