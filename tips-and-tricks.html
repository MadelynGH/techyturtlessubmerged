Tips and Tricks

You can help too! Here are some things you can do to help:

  1. Try not to put fertilizer in right before it rains to reduce runoff.
  2. If you're getting new grass or plants, try to get some that need less fertilizer. Try to use natural fertilizers as well, such as dung or compost.
  3. Spread the word! Tell your friends and family about the problem!
  4. Try to get fertilizer that contains a safe amount of nitrogen and/or phosphorous.
  5. When applying fertilizer, mix it well! Make sure it's nicely underground.
